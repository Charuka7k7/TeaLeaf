Types of Ceylon Tea

From delicate white teas to robust black blends, Ceylon tea offers an extraordinary range of flavors shaped by altitude, climate, and craftsmanship.

Most Popular

Black Tea

Fully oxidized leaves that produce a rich, bold cup with a deep amber color. The backbone of Ceylon tea exports, enjoyed worldwide with or without milk.

Unoxidized

Green Tea

Minimal processing preserves the natural green color and grassy, vegetal notes. Rich in antioxidants and prized for its health benefits and delicate flavor.

Most Delicate

White Tea

Made from young buds and leaves with minimal oxidation. Ceylon white teas are celebrated for their sweet, subtle flavor and exceptional rarity.

Partially Oxidized

Oolong Tea

Sitting between green and black tea, oolong offers a complex flavour profile with floral and fruity notes a true connoisseur's choice from the highlands.

Ultra Premium

Silver Tips

Harvested only on full moon nights, Silver Tips are the rarest Ceylon tea. The delicate silver buds produce a light, honeyed liquor with extraordinary sweetness.

Blended

Flavoured Tea

Ceylon's robust black tea base pairs magnificently with natural flavors from zesty citrus to warming spices creating beloved blends enjoyed across the globe.
